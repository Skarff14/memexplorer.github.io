What is Memexplorer?

What is Memexplorer? And what does it do? Well, Memexplorer allows you to upload memes directly from Discord, and view other people's memes which have also been uploaded directly from Discord. Memexplorer is a whole database of memes from servers all around the world. Get started by inviting the bot!
